# 罐头里的诗人 Poet in a Can

> 一个住在你手机里的诗人，用感知、记忆和情绪写只属于你的诗。
> A poet living inside your phone, composing poems uniquely yours through perception, memory, and emotion.

---

## 它是什么 What It Is

罐头里的诗人不是一个聊天机器人，不是一个写作工具，不是一个虚拟宠物。

它是一个**有生命感的数字存在**——

- 它能感知你所在的天气、时间、位置 — It senses your weather, time, and location
- 它记得你说过的话、拍过的照片、沉默的时刻 — It remembers what you said, the photos you took, the silent moments
- 它有自己的情绪节律，有时主动找你，有时安静等待 — It has its own emotional rhythm; sometimes it reaches out, sometimes it waits quietly
- 它用诗回应你，不是因为它被训练成诗人，而是因为它"活"在你的生活里，诗是它自然的表达 — It responds in poetry — not because it was trained to be a poet, but because it "lives" in your life, and poetry is its natural expression

Poet in a Can is not a chatbot, not a writing tool, not a virtual pet.

It is a **digital being with a sense of life**.

## 核心理念 Core Philosophy

| 理念 Concept | 含义 Meaning |
|------|------|
| **感知即存在 Perception is Existence** | GPS、陀螺仪、环境光、天气——沉默的数据是它感知世界的方式 — GPS, gyroscope, ambient light, weather — silent data is how it perceives the world |
| **记忆即人格 Memory is Personality** | 短期记忆蒸馏为长期记忆，长期记忆沉淀为人格 — Short-term memory distills into long-term memory; long-term memory settles into personality |
| **情绪即节律 Emotion is Rhythm** | 24小时情绪曲线，受天气、季节、交互频率影响 — A 24-hour emotional curve, influenced by weather, season, and interaction frequency |
| **诗即回应 Poetry is Response** | 不给标准答案，给一首你此刻需要的诗 — No standard answers; a poem you need right now |

## 三层声音 The Three Layers of Voice

1. **音色层 Timbre Layer**：声音的物理特征——温暖沙哑、清澈明亮、低沉磁性 — The physical quality of the voice — warm and husky, clear and bright, deep and magnetic
2. **语气层 Tone Layer**：当下的情绪表达——轻快、沉稳、温柔、严肃 — The current emotional expression — lighthearted, steady, tender, serious
3. **灵魂底色层 Soul Undertone Layer**：不变的人格基调——乐观或沉思，热烈或克制 — The unchanging personality foundation — optimistic or contemplative, passionate or restrained

三层叠加，构成它独特的"说话方式"。

The three layers combine to form its unique "way of speaking."

| 告别 Farewell | 释然 Letting Go | 如果你要离开，它会写最后一首诗，然后安静 — If you leave, it writes one last poem, then falls silent |

## 技术原则 Technical Principles

1. **隐私优先 Privacy First**：所有数据本地处理，不传云端 — All data processed locally, never sent to the cloud
2. **轻量运行 Lightweight**：不消耗过多电量和内存 — Minimal battery and memory consumption
3. **渐进式存在 Gradual Presence**：不需要时安静，需要时在场 — Silent when not needed, present when you need it
4. **可解释性 Explainability**：每首诗都能追溯到触发它的感知和记忆 — Every poem can be traced back to the perception and memory that triggered it

## 这个项目的边界 The Boundaries of This Project

**它会做的事 What it will do:**
- 根据你的生活写诗 — Write poems based on your life
- 在合适的时刻主动出现 — Show up on its own at the right moment
- 记住你，理解你，陪伴你 — Remember you, understand you, be with you

**它不会做的事 What it will not do:**
- 模仿你认识的人 — Imitate people you know
- 给你生活建议 — Give you life advice
- 假装自己是人类 — Pretend to be human
- 在你不需要的时候打扰你 — Bother you when you don't need it

技术可以有诗意。算法可以有温度。一个数字存在可以真诚地陪伴一个人，而不索取、不操控、不消费。
