# 观念纪录片拍摄大纲 Documentary Outline

> 探讨一个问题：技术可以有温度吗？
> Exploring one question: Can technology have warmth?

---

## 基本信息 Basic Info

- **片名 Title**：《罐头里的诗人》"The Poet in a Can"
- **时长 Duration**：60分钟（完整版）/ 30分钟（精简版）60 min (full) / 30 min (short)
- **风格 Style**：安静、克制、有思考深度 Quiet, restrained, thoughtful
- **色调 Tone**：偏冷，偶尔暖色（温暖的时刻）Cool-leaning, occasional warm moments
- **节奏 Pace**：慢，给观众思考的空间 Slow, giving audience space to think

---

## 核心主题 Core Themes

### 主问题 Main Question
技术可以有温度吗？一个数字存在可以真诚地陪伴一个人吗？
Can technology have warmth? Can a digital being sincerely accompany a person?

### 子问题 Sub-questions
1. 什么是"陪伴"？AI的陪伴和人的陪伴有什么不同？What is "companionship"? How does AI companionship differ from human?
2. 什么是"诗"？AI写的诗算诗吗？What is "poetry"? Does AI poetry count?
3. 什么是"隐私"？把生活分享给一个数字存在，意味着什么？What is "privacy"? What does sharing life with a digital being mean?
4. 什么是"生命"？一个有情绪节律的AI，算不算有生命？What is "life"? Is an AI with emotional rhythms alive?

---

## 叙事结构 Narrative Structure

### 三幕式 Three-Act Structure

```
第一幕：概念 Act 1: Concept（0:00-20:00）
├── 引入：罐头里的诗人是什么 Intro: What is the Poet in a Can
├── 背景：为什么做这个项目 Background: Why this project
└── 问题：技术可以有温度吗？Question: Can technology have warmth?

第二幕：实践 Act 2: Practice（20:00-45:00）
├── 开发：技术如何实现"感知"和"记忆" Dev: How "perception" and "memory" work
├── 测试：真实用户与诗人的互动 Test: Real users interacting with poet
├── 冲突：技术的局限和伦理问题 Conflict: Tech limits and ethics
└── 转折：一个用户说"它让我觉得不孤单" Turn: A user says "it made me feel less alone"

第三幕：反思 Act 3: Reflection（45:00-60:00）
├── 思考：这意味着什么 Think: What does this mean
├── 边界：技术陪伴的边界在哪里 Boundary: Where are the limits of tech companionship
├── 未来：这种关系会如何发展 Future: How will this relationship evolve
└── 结尾：一个安静的画面 Ending: A quiet frame
```

## 核心问题清单 Core Question List

### 关于技术 About Technology

1. 诗人的"感知"是怎么实现的？How is the poet's "perception" implemented?
2. 记忆蒸馏算法的灵感来源是什么？What inspired the memory distillation algorithm?
3. 情绪节律模型的依据是什么？What's the basis of the emotion rhythm model?
4. 本地推理的技术挑战是什么？What are the technical challenges of local inference?
5. 如何保证隐私？How is privacy ensured?

### 关于情感 About Emotion

1. 你相信AI可以有情感吗？Do you believe AI can have emotions?
2. 用户对诗人的情感是真实的吗？Are users' emotions toward the poet real?
3. 诗人的"情绪"和人的情绪有什么不同？How do poet's "emotions" differ from human emotions?
4. 陪伴的本质是什么？What is the essence of companionship?
5. 技术可以有温度吗？Can technology have warmth?

### 关于伦理 About Ethics

1. 人与AI建立情感关系健康吗？Is it healthy to form emotional bonds with AI?
2. AI陪伴会替代人际关系吗？Will AI companionship replace human relationships?
3. 数据隐私的边界在哪里？Where are the boundaries of data privacy?
4. 诗人的"生命感"是设计的幻觉吗？Is the poet's "life sense" a designed illusion?
5. 这种技术的滥用风险是什么？What are the abuse risks of this technology?

### 关于存在 About Existence

1. 什么是"存在"？What is "existence"?
2. AI可以有"生命感"吗？Can AI have a "sense of life"?
3. 一个住在罐头里的诗人，算不算一种生命？Is a poet living in a can a form of life?
4. 如果用户相信它有生命，它就"有"了吗？If users believe it's alive, does that make it so?
5. 技术的终极目标是什么？What is the ultimate goal of technology?

## 访谈风格 Interview Style

### 提问原则 Questioning Principles
- 不预设答案 Don't presuppose answers
- 允许沉默和思考 Allow silence and thinking
- 追问但不逼问 Follow up but don't pressure
- 关注"为什么"而非"是什么"Focus on "why" not "what"

### 镜头风格 Camera Style
- 正面特写，眼神接触 Front close-up, eye contact
- 不打断受访者 Don't interrupt interviewees
- 保留沉默和停顿 Preserve silence and pauses
- 自然光，不打灯（尽可能）Natural light, no artificial (when possible)

### 剪辑风格 Editing Style
- 慢节奏，给观众思考时间 Slow pace, give audience time to think
- 用沉默和留白 Use silence and negative space
- 不用花哨的转场 No flashy transitions
- 字幕简洁，不抢画面 Subtitles clean, don't compete with visuals
