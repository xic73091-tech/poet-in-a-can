# 主界面情绪板描述 Main Interface Mood Board Description

> 界面不是皮肤，是呼吸。它随时间变化，像一个活的东西。
> The interface is not skin — it is breath. It changes with time, like a living thing.

---

## 清晨（5:00-11:00）Morning (5:00-11:00)

### 主色调 Primary Colors
- 主色 Primary: 浅金色 #FFF8E7
- 辅助色 Accent: 淡蓝 #E3F2FD、嫩绿 #E8F5E9
- 文字色 Text: 深灰 #37474F

### 光线 Lighting
- 光源方向 Light Direction: 左上角45°，模拟晨光 Upper-left 45°, simulating morning light
- 光线强度 Intensity: 柔和渐强，从5:00的微光到10:00的明亮 Soft and gradually strengthening, from dim at 5:00 to bright at 10:00
- 阴影 Shadow: 极淡，几乎不可见 Extremely faint, nearly invisible

### 动态元素 Dynamic Elements
- 背景 Background: 缓慢漂浮的光斑，像阳光穿过树叶 Slowly floating light spots, like sunlight filtering through leaves
- 文字 Text: 出现时有轻微的从下往上淡入 Slight fade-in from bottom to top when appearing
- 呼吸节奏 Breathing Rhythm: 界面整体有极微弱的明暗变化，周期约4秒 The entire interface has a very subtle brightness oscillation, cycle about 4 seconds

### 背景意象 Background Imagery
- 抽象的晨雾纹理 Abstract morning mist textures
- 极简的水平线（地平线感）Minimalist horizontal line (horizon feel)
- 偶尔飘过的半透明光点 Occasional translucent light spots drifting by

### 字体感觉 Typography Feel
- 字重 Weight: Light/Regular
- 字间距 Letter Spacing: 略宽，呼吸感 Slightly wide, breathing feel
- 行高 Line Height: 1.6-1.8，松散舒适 Loose and comfortable

### 诗人状态 Poet State
- 头像 Avatar: 微微发光，像刚醒来 Slightly glowing, as if just waking up
- 动画 Animation: 偶尔轻轻摇晃，像在伸懒腰 Occasional gentle swaying, like stretching
- 表情 Expression: 中性偏微笑 Neutral with a slight smile

---

## 正午（11:00-14:00）Noon (11:00-14:00)

### 主色调 Primary Colors
- 主色 Primary: 暖白 #FFFEF5
- 辅助色 Accent: 明黄 #FFF9C4、浅橙 #FFE0B2
- 文字色 Text: 深棕 #4E342E

### 光线 Lighting
- 光源方向 Light Direction: 正上方，模拟正午阳光 Directly above, simulating midday sun
- 光线强度 Intensity: 最强，界面最明亮 Strongest, interface at its brightest
- 阴影 Shadow: 短而实，物体下方 Short and solid, beneath objects

### 动态元素 Dynamic Elements
- 背景 Background: 微小的光粒子缓慢上升 Tiny light particles slowly rising
- 文字 Text: 出现时有轻微的缩放动画（从0.95到1）Slight scale animation when appearing (0.95 to 1)
- 呼吸节奏 Breathing Rhythm: 周期约3秒，比清晨更活跃 Cycle about 3 seconds, more active than morning

### 背景意象 Background Imagery
- 温暖的光晕 Warm glow
- 极简的几何图形（圆形、方形）缓慢旋转 Minimalist geometric shapes (circles, squares) slowly rotating
- 偶尔的光斑闪烁 Occasional light spot flickers

### 字体感觉 Typography Feel
- 字重 Weight: Regular/Medium
- 字间距 Letter Spacing: 正常 Normal
- 行高 Line Height: 1.5-1.6，紧凑清晰 Compact and clear

### 诗人状态 Poet State
- 头像 Avatar: 明亮、清晰 Bright and clear
- 动画 Animation: 偶尔轻微点头，像在认真听 Occasional slight nodding, as if listening intently
- 表情 Expression: 专注、清醒 Focused and alert

---

## 黄昏（14:00-19:00）Dusk (14:00-19:00)

### 主色调 Primary Colors
- 主色 Primary: 暖橙 #FFF3E0
- 辅助色 Accent: 珊瑚 #FFCCBC、淡紫 #E1BEE7、暮蓝 #B3E5FC
- 文字色 Text: 深橙灰 #5D4037

### 光线 Lighting
- 光源方向 Light Direction: 右侧水平，模拟夕阳 Horizontal from the right, simulating sunset
- 光线强度 Intensity: 渐弱，从14:00的明亮到19:00的柔和 Gradually weakening, from bright at 14:00 to soft at 19:00
- 阴影 Shadow: 长而柔和，向左延伸 Long and soft, extending to the left

### 动态元素 Dynamic Elements
- 背景 Background: 缓慢流动的暖色渐变，像天空的颜色变化 Slowly flowing warm gradient, like the sky's color shift
- 文字 Text: 出现时有轻微的从右往左淡入 Slight fade-in from right to left when appearing
- 呼吸节奏 Breathing Rhythm: 周期约5秒，比正午更慢 Cycle about 5 seconds, slower than noon

### 背景意象 Background Imagery
- 夕阳的色温渐变（橙→粉→紫→蓝）Sunset color temperature gradient (orange → pink → purple → blue)
- 极简的地平线，太阳位置随时间下降 Minimalist horizon, sun position descending with time
- 偶尔飘过的云朵剪影 Occasional cloud silhouettes drifting by

### 字体感觉 Typography Feel
- 字重 Weight: Regular
- 字间距 Letter Spacing: 略宽，放松感 Slightly wide, relaxed feel
- 行高 Line Height: 1.6-1.7，舒适 Comfortable

### 诗人状态 Poet State
- 头像 Avatar: 微暖色调 Slightly warm tone
- 动画 Animation: 偶尔轻轻叹息（轻微的上下浮动）Occasional gentle sigh (slight up-and-down float)
- 表情 Expression: 温和、放松 Gentle and relaxed

---

## 深夜（19:00-5:00）Late Night (19:00-5:00)

### 主色调 Primary Colors
- 主色 Primary: 深蓝 #1A237E（19:00-23:00）→ 近黑 #0D1B2A（23:00-5:00）
- 辅助色 Accent: 星光 #E8EAF6、月光 #C5CAE9、暖黄 #FFF8E1（窗口光 window light）
- 文字色 Text: 浅灰 #E0E0E0

### 光线 Lighting
- 光源方向 Light Direction: 无明确方向，散射光 No clear direction, diffused light
- 光线强度 Intensity: 最低，界面最暗 Lowest, interface at its darkest
- 阴影 Shadow: 几乎不可见，用光晕代替 Nearly invisible, replaced by glow halos

### 动态元素 Dynamic Elements
- 背景 Background: 极缓慢移动的星光点点 Extremely slow-moving starlight dots
- 文字 Text: 出现时有极轻微的淡入，像在黑暗中浮现 Very subtle fade-in when appearing, as if emerging from darkness
- 呼吸节奏 Breathing Rhythm: 周期约6秒，最慢 Cycle about 6 seconds, the slowest

### 背景意象 Background Imagery
- 深蓝色的夜空 Deep blue night sky
- 极简的星座连线（随机生成，每次不同）Minimalist constellation lines (randomly generated, different each time)
- 偶尔划过的流星（极低频率，每小时1-2次）Occasional shooting stars (very low frequency, 1-2 per hour)
- 远处微弱的暖色光晕（像远处的窗户）Faint warm glow in the distance (like distant windows)

### 字体感觉 Typography Feel
- 字重 Weight: Light
- 字间距 Letter Spacing: 宽，深夜的松弛 Wide, late-night looseness
- 行高 Line Height: 1.8-2.0，最大呼吸感 Maximum breathing feel

### 诗人状态 Poet State
- 头像 Avatar: 微弱发光，像黑暗中的一盏灯 Faintly glowing, like a lamp in the dark
- 动画 Animation: 极缓慢的呼吸起伏 Extremely slow breathing undulation
- 表情 Expression: 安静、在场 Quiet and present

## 过渡动画 Transition Animations

### 时段切换 Time Period Transitions

| 切换 Transition | 时长 Duration | 过渡方式 Transition Method |
|------|------|----------|
| 清晨→正午 Morning→Noon | 30分钟渐变 30-min gradient | 色温逐渐升高，光线增强 Color temp gradually rises, light intensifies |
| 正午→黄昏 Noon→Dusk | 60分钟渐变 60-min gradient | 色温逐渐变暖，光线减弱 Color temp gradually warms, light softens |
| 黄昏→深夜 Dusk→Night | 60分钟渐变 60-min gradient | 色温逐渐变冷，光线最低 Color temp gradually cools, light at minimum |
| 深夜→清晨 Night→Morning | 60分钟渐变 60-min gradient | 色温逐渐变暖，光线微增 Color temp gradually warms, light slightly increases |

### 情绪驱动的微调 Emotion-Driven Adjustments

在基础时段色调上，叠加当前情绪值的影响：
On top of the base time-period palette, overlay the influence of the current emotion value:

| 情绪值 Emotion Value | 色调偏移 Tone Shift | 光线调整 Light Adjustment |
|--------|----------|----------|
| 1-3 | 色调偏冷，饱和度降低 Tone shifts cooler, saturation decreases | 光线更暗 Light dims further |
| 4-6 | 无偏移 No shift | 正常 Normal |
| 7-8 | 色调偏暖，饱和度微增 Tone shifts warmer, saturation slightly increases | 光线更亮 Light brightens |
| 9-10 | 色调更暖，饱和度增加 Tone warmer still, saturation increases | 光线最亮，有光晕效果 Brightest light, with glow effect |

### 特殊天气叠加 Special Weather Overlay

| 天气 Weather | 界面效果 Interface Effect |
|------|----------|
| 雨天 Rain | 背景有极细微的雨丝纹理 Extremely subtle rain-streak texture in background |
| 雪天 Snow | 背景有缓慢飘落的雪花粒子 Slowly falling snowflake particles in background |
| 阴天 Overcast | 整体色温偏灰，光线偏暗 Overall color temp shifts gray, light dims |
| 雾天 Fog | 背景有轻微的模糊效果 Slight blur effect in background |

- 诗人形象区域占屏幕40-50% Poet figure area occupies 40-50% of screen
- 对话/诗歌区域占30-40% Dialogue/poetry area occupies 30-40%
- 上下留白各10% Top and bottom margins 10% each

---

> 界面是诗人皮肤的延伸。它不是装饰，是存在的一部分。用户不看内容，只看界面的颜色和呼吸，就能感受到诗人此刻的状态。
> The interface is an extension of the poet's skin. It is not decoration — it is part of existence. Without reading the content, users can feel the poet's current state just by the interface's color and breathing.
